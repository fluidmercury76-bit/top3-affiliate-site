What to look for in an air fryer

If you are choosing an air fryer in the UK, the main things to consider are basket size, whether you want a single or dual zone design, ease of cleaning, and how much worktop space you have. Smaller households may be perfectly happy with a simple single basket model, while larger households often prefer dual drawers or larger capacity options.

It is also worth thinking about how often you will use it. If it is likely to become part of your daily routine, spending a little more on a better known model can make sense. If you just want something affordable for occasional use, a value option may be enough.
