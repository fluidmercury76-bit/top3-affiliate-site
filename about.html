About Top 3 Choices

Hi, I'm Tim McGregor. Top 3 Choices started as a personal notes project after too many expensive mistakes buying products that looked great online but were frustrating in real life.

Why I built this site

I kept seeing the same pattern: most roundups repeated manufacturer features, but very few explained what ownership feels like after week two or month three. So I started writing comparisons that answer practical questions first, like noise in a bedroom, running cost over time, and whether something is actually suited to a small flat.

My experience and process

For years I have helped friends, family, and clients shortlist home and lifestyle products. My process is simple: compare measurable differences, stress-test use-case fit, and call out trade-offs clearly. If a product is only good for a narrow scenario, I say that. If a cheaper option is genuinely better value for most people, that becomes the pick.

What “Top 3” means here

  • Best overall: strongest balance for most buyers.
  • Best value: best practical return for cost-conscious buyers.
  • Best premium: worth paying more only when the upgrade is meaningful.

Editorial standards

I focus on clarity over hype: who each product is for, who should skip it, and why. You will also see internal guide clusters (choose / running-cost / troubleshooting) so you can go deeper before buying.
