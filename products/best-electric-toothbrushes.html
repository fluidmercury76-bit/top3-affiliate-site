Frequently asked questions

Are electric toothbrushes better than manual toothbrushes?

Many people find electric toothbrushes easier for thorough, consistent brushing, especially if they want timers, pressure sensors or different cleaning modes.

How often should I change the brush head?

Most brush heads should be replaced every few months, or sooner if the bristles start to wear out.

Do expensive electric toothbrushes clean better?

More expensive models often add extra features, but a well designed mid range toothbrush can still give very good cleaning performance.

Are pressure sensors useful?

Yes, pressure sensors can help protect gums by warning you if you are brushing too hard.

Can children use electric toothbrushes?

Some electric toothbrushes are designed specifically for children, with softer heads and simpler settings. It is best to choose one suited to their age.
