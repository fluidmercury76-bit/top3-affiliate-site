Frequently asked questions

Are digital bathroom scales more accurate than analogue ones?

Digital scales are usually easier to read and more consistent for day to day use. Analogue scales can work well too, but digital models tend to offer better precision.

Should I weigh myself at the same time each day?

Yes, weighing yourself at the same time under similar conditions gives the most useful comparison. Many people prefer first thing in the morning.

Do smart bathroom scales really help?

Smart scales can be useful if you want app tracking and long term trends. If you only want a simple weight reading, a standard digital scale is often enough.

Can bathroom scales work on carpet?

Most scales give more reliable readings on a hard, flat surface. Carpet can affect accuracy, so tiles or solid flooring are usually best.

How often should I replace bathroom scales?

If the readings remain consistent and the display works properly, good scales can last for years. Replace them if readings drift, the platform becomes unstable or the screen fails.
