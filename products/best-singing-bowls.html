How to choose the right option

The most important factor is size and sound. Smaller bowls are easier to use and better for beginners, while larger bowls produce deeper tones and longer resonance.

Handmade bowls often feel more authentic and varied in tone, while machine made options are more consistent. The best choice depends on whether you want ease of use or a more immersive sound experience.
