Frequently asked questions

What size dehumidifier do I need?

The right size depends on the room and the level of damp or condensation. Small rooms may only need a compact model, while larger or wetter spaces need more extraction power.

Do dehumidifiers help with condensation?

Yes, a good dehumidifier can reduce moisture in the air and help tackle condensation on windows, walls and colder surfaces.

Are dehumidifiers expensive to run?

Running costs vary by model and use time, but many people find them worthwhile for improving comfort, protecting belongings and helping laundry dry indoors.

Can a dehumidifier help dry clothes indoors?

Yes, many models are very useful for indoor drying, especially in colder months when opening windows is less practical.

Where should I place a dehumidifier?

Place it where moisture is most noticeable, such as near laundry, condensation prone windows or a damp room, while leaving enough space around it for airflow.
