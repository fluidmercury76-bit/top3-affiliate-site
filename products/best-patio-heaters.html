How to choose the right option

The best patio heater depends on your space and how you use it. Gas heaters are better for large open areas, while electric and infrared heaters work better in smaller or covered spaces.

Electric models are easier to run and maintain, while gas heaters provide stronger heat. Infrared heaters are often the most efficient because they heat people directly rather than the surrounding air.

For most buyers, a pyramid gas patio heater is the best overall option because it delivers strong and reliable heat. An electric patio heater is a better value choice for smaller spaces, while an infrared patio heater is the stronger premium option for efficient and modern outdoor heating.
