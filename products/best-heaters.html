How to choose the right option

The best heater for you depends on the size of the room, how often you use it, and whether you want fast heat or slower more even warmth. Fan and convector heaters are often better for quick bursts of heat, while oil filled and panel heaters can feel better for longer steady use.

If you use this kind of product regularly, spending a little more can make sense if it improves comfort, design or controls. If you only need something for occasional heating, the better value option may be enough.
