Frequently asked questions

What should I look for in wireless earbuds?

Comfort, sound quality, battery life and connection reliability are the main things to consider. Some people will also want strong noise cancelling.

Are noise cancelling earbuds worth it?

If you travel, commute or work in busy spaces, noise cancelling can make a noticeable difference to comfort and listening quality.

How long should wireless earbuds last on one charge?

Battery life varies, but most good earbuds should offer enough playback for daily use, with the charging case adding extra top ups.

Can wireless earbuds be good for calls as well as music?

Yes, many modern earbuds handle calls well, but microphone quality can vary a lot between models.

Do all wireless earbuds fit the same?

No, fit varies by shape and tip size. A secure and comfortable fit is important for sound quality and longer listening sessions.
