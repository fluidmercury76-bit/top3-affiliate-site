Frequently asked questions

What makes a good desk lamp?

A good desk lamp should provide clear light without glare, be easy to position and suit the way you work, read or study.

Is adjustable brightness important in a desk lamp?

Yes, adjustable brightness helps you create a more comfortable setup for different tasks and times of day.

Are LED desk lamps worth it?

LED desk lamps are popular because they are efficient, long lasting and usually stay cooler than older bulb types.

Should I choose warm or cool light for a desk lamp?

Warm light can feel calmer, while cooler light may feel sharper for focused work. The most flexible option is a lamp with adjustable colour temperature.

How bright should a desk lamp be?

It should be bright enough for comfortable reading or work without causing eye strain. The best level depends on your room lighting and the task you are doing.
