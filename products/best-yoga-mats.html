How to choose the right option

The best yoga mat for you depends on how much grip you need, how much cushioning you prefer, and where you practise. Thicker mats can feel more comfortable for knees and joints, while firmer mats can feel more stable for balance and standing poses.

If you practise regularly, spending a little more can make sense if it improves grip, comfort or durability. If you only need something for occasional sessions at home, the better value option may be enough.
