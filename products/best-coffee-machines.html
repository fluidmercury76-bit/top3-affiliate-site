Frequently asked questions

What type of coffee machine is best for home use?

The best type depends on how involved you want to be. Pod machines are simple, bean to cup models are convenient, and manual machines offer more control.

Are bean to cup coffee machines worth it?

They can be worth it if you drink coffee regularly and want fresh coffee with less effort than a manual espresso setup.

How much should I spend on a coffee machine?

A lower budget can still get you a decent machine, but spending more often brings better consistency, stronger build quality and more drink options.

Do coffee machines need a lot of cleaning?

All coffee machines need regular cleaning, but some are easier than others. Machines with automatic rinsing and removable parts are usually more convenient.

Can I make plant based coffees with a home machine?

Yes, many machines work well with oat, soy or almond milk, especially if they have a steam wand or milk frothing system designed for different textures.
