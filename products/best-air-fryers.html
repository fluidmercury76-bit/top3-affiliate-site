How to choose the right air fryer size

A smaller single basket model is usually enough for one person or a couple cooking lighter meals, snacks or side dishes. Once you start cooking full dinners more often, a roomier basket becomes much more useful because you can fit chicken, chips or vegetables without crowding the drawer.

If you often cook for more than two people, batch cook, or want to keep foods separate, moving up to a larger single basket or a dual basket model is usually worth it. In many kitchens, the real question is not just capacity, but whether you have the worktop space to leave the fryer out ready to use.

Air fryer running costs vs oven

For smaller meals, an air fryer is often cheaper to run than a full size oven because it heats up faster and cooks in a smaller chamber. That makes it especially useful for quick lunches, evening meals for one or two people, reheating leftovers and cooking frozen food without warming the whole kitchen.

A full oven still makes more sense for large trays, batch baking and bigger family cooking. But for everyday portions, many people find an air fryer gives them a better balance of speed, convenience and lower energy use.

Are dual basket air fryers worth it?

Dual basket models are worth considering if you regularly cook two foods together, such as chips and chicken, or vegetables and a main. They also help when different foods need different timings, because you do not have to stop and reshuffle halfway through.

That said, not everyone needs one. If you mainly cook simple meals for one or two people, a good single basket model can still be the better value choice. Dual basket air fryers earn their keep most clearly in busy households where flexibility matters more than saving space.

Frequently asked questions

What size air fryer do I need?

A compact single basket air fryer usually suits one or two people, while larger single basket or dual basket models are better for couples who batch cook and for family meals.

Are air fryers cheaper to run than ovens?

For smaller meals, air fryers are often cheaper to run than a full oven because they heat quickly, cook faster and warm a much smaller space.

Can an air fryer replace an oven?

It can replace an oven for many everyday meals, reheating and crisping jobs, but a full oven is still better for large trays, batch baking and bigger family cooking.

Are dual basket air fryers worth it?

They are worth it if you often cook two foods at once, want different timings or need more flexibility for family meals.

How easy are air fryers to clean?

Most are straightforward to clean if the basket has a good non stick coating and the removable parts are dishwasher safe.
