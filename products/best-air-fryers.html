Frequently asked questions

What size air fryer do I need?

A small household may be happy with a compact model, while families usually benefit from a larger drawer or dual basket design. Think about how many people you cook for most often.

Are air fryers cheaper to run than ovens?

Air fryers are often cheaper to run for smaller meals because they heat up quickly and use less power over a shorter cooking time than a full size oven.

Can an air fryer replace an oven?

For many everyday meals it can handle roasting, reheating and crisping very well, but a full oven is still better for large trays, batch baking and bigger family cooking.

Are dual basket air fryers worth it?

Dual basket models are very useful if you want to cook two foods at once or keep different flavours separate. They are especially handy for family meals.

How easy are air fryers to clean?

Most modern air fryers are easy to clean if the basket and tray have a good non stick coating. Dishwasher safe parts can make cleaning even simpler.
