Troubleshooting air fryers: ordered checks

Specific product context

2–6 portions per cycle is typical home use.

Practical numbers and examples

  • 3.5–9L baskets are common.
  • 1.4–2.4kW typical draw.
  • 48–65dB typical noise.

Troubleshooting steps (in order)

  1. Confirm setup/power and reset if relevant.
  2. Clean key contact/flow/wear points.
  3. Run one controlled retest cycle.
  4. If fault repeats, move to repair or replacement.
